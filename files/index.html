Meteorite, Star and Rocket

This game is inspired by the game Stone, Paper and Scissors

Rules

The game is played in 5 rounds, the goal is to reach 3 points to win. You will have to choose between 3 objects that will face an AI

  • The Meteorite that beats the Rocket
  • The Star that beats the Meteorite
  • The Rocket that beats the Star
